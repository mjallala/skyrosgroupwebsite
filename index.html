About Skyros Group

Exquisite Quality you can Taste

Born of an entrepreneurial spirit and worldly vision, the founders of Skyros Group seek to bring the finest the world has to offer to your table. We seek out exceptional and distinct products from around the world to capture the exquisite culinary experience.


Our mission is to seek out new and exciting products from international sources and introduce them to the local market. We procure delicacies of the highest quality that fit with the current trend of holistic nutritious food with natural ingredients.
